What is Weather?

Introduction

Weather is a word people use daily as you can hear in the news, conversations or just in your thoughts before going out to play. I even have a strong feeling that checking the weather is one of the first things you do every morning, specially during winter to see if it's snowing so you can finally have a snowball fight!

Have you even thought what Weather really means? I believe that for any respected scientist, it's incredibly important to know the definition of what you want to study in order to better understand it.

According to Oxford dictionary, Weather is:

The state of the atmosphere at a particular place and time as regards heat, cloudiness, dryness, sunshine, wind, rain, etc.

Explanation

Now that we know the definition, lets break it down into two parts to fully understand it shall we.

First part: The 'state of the atmosphere' at a particular place and time. This means is that weather is the description (a synonym for 'state') of the atmosphere's behaviour in some place and some time.

Second part: 'as regards heat, cloudiness, dryness, sunshine, wind, rain, etc.' are properties that describe the 'behaviour of the atmosphere' or Weather.
